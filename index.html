About Us

Two Schools.
One Mission.

We are a group of students coming together through QE Together, a collaboration between Queen Elizabeth's School, Barnet and Queen Elizabeth's Girls' School. Our shared goal is simple: to make a meaningful difference in our community.

This bake sale is more than just an event — it's a collective effort to address real challenges within our local area. From supporting families in need to contributing to community projects, we are committed to using our time, skills, and creativity to create positive change.

By working together, we aim to raise funds that directly support local initiatives while also bringing people closer as a community. Every bake, every donation, and every act of support helps us take a step toward solving problems and building something better for everyone. Together, we believe small actions can lead to big change.

Our Cause

Why We Bake & Give

Every cupcake sold helps fund meaningful change in our local community. Here's where your generosity goes:

```
🏘️

Local Community Projects

Supporting grassroots initiatives across High Barnet — from neighbourhood clean-ups to community garden programmes that bring residents together.

👵

Supporting the Elderly

Partnering with local care homes and charities to provide companionship, activities, and vital support for isolated elderly residents in our borough.

🌱

Youth Volunteering

Funding opportunities for young people to volunteer locally — developing skills, building confidence, and creating a culture of giving back from an early age.

```
